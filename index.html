Ways to Apply

Apply as a Freshman >>

Freshman applicants

are current high school
students (with or without college credits) or high
school graduates who have not earned any college
credits since high school graduation.

Apply as a Graduate Student >>

Graduate applicants have an undergraduate degree
and are looking to further their education with a
master's, doctoral or professional degree.

Apply as a Transfer Student >>

Transfer Applicant

are students who have graduated
from high school and have enrolled in a post-
secondary institution (with or without credits earned)
during a fall or spring semester.

Apply as a International Student >>

International applicants

are not citizens or
permanent residents of the United States. They also
have not graduated or will not graduate from a
Texas high school after three years of residence in
Texas.
